Riverside is located in southern California in the heart of the region known as the Inland Empire. The city is known for its once expansive orange fields. Riverside was established in 1883 and currently has a population of over 315,000 residents. Riverside is classified as urban and the average household income is $96,476. The state's average income is $119,149.
